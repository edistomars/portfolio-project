About me.

Hi. I'm Marla Laystrom, a front-end developer based in Indiana .
I have been taking courses through SheCodes since May 2021 and have obtained certifications in Introduction to Coding and Front-End Development. I'm currently working on the Responsive Web Development certification and will follow that up with the React Development certification. I really enjoy this world of coding. To learn, create and then see it unfold on websites is where it's at for me!

They have taught me to appreciate Sci-Fi, video and board games, and the glorious library . We also share a love of travel and exploring . Then there is green tea , it is by my side in those early and busy hours each day.
